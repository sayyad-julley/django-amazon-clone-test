## Bulk Product Selection Feature

The product list page includes a "Select All" checkbox functionality that allows administrators to quickly select or deselect multiple products.

### Features
- A "Select All" checkbox in the table header
- Individual checkboxes for each product
- Instant selection/deselection of all products

### Usage
1. Click the "Select All Products" checkbox at the top of the product list
2. All product checkboxes will be checked or unchecked accordingly
3. You can also manually check/uncheck individual product checkboxes

### Implementation Details
- Located in: `DjangoEcommerceApp/templates/admin_templates/product_list.html`
- JavaScript handles the checkbox toggle logic
- Provides a convenient way to perform bulk actions on products
